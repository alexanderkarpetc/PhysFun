BINNY - THE RECYCLING ROBOT FOLLOWER
=====================================

OVERVIEW
--------
Binny is a funny, sarcastic flying robot companion for the 2D platformer game. 
He follows the player and serves as a mobile recycling unit with a big personality.

NAME ORIGIN
-----------
Full Name: "Binbot" (shortened to "Binny")
- A play on "Bin" (trash/recycling bin) + "Bot" (robot)
- The name reflects his primary function: collecting and recycling resources

DESIGN & APPEARANCE
-------------------
- Flying Robot: Hovers and follows the player using propulsion systems
- Big Hole on Top: Large opening/receptacle on the top of the robot where players 
  can deposit collected resources
- Stupid Funny Face: Features a comical, expressive face with:
  - Exaggerated eyes (possibly googly or animated)
  - Silly mouth/expression
  - Overall goofy, endearing appearance
- Compact body design suitable for a 2D side-scrolling platformer

FUNCTIONALITY
-------------
- Resource Collection: Players can throw/drop collected resources into Binny's 
  top opening
- Recycling System: Automatically processes and recycles collected resources
- Mobile Companion: Follows the player throughout the game world
- Flying Mechanics: Hovers at a comfortable distance from the player, adjusting 
  position as needed

PERSONALITY & DIALOGUE
----------------------
- Personality: Sarcastic, talks "shit" (playful trash-talk, not offensive)
- Communication Method: VOICE/SOUND ONLY (no text dialogue)
  - Uses sound effects and voice clips for communication
  - Appropriate for fast-paced platformer gameplay
  - Doesn't interrupt gameplay flow with text reading
- Dialogue Style: 
  - Snarky comments
  - Funny observations
  - Encouraging but sarcastic remarks
  - Reactions to player actions (collecting items, taking damage, etc.)

GAMEPLAY INTEGRATION
-------------------
- Follows player automatically
- Resource deposit interaction (player throws items into top hole)
- Visual feedback when recycling (animations, particles, etc.)
- Audio feedback for all interactions
- Does not obstruct player movement or platforming

TECHNICAL CONSIDERATIONS
------------------------
- 2D Sprite/Animation system
- Flying AI that follows player with smooth movement
- Collision detection for resource collection
- Sound system for voice lines
- Recycling processing system (converts resources to usable materials/currency)

VOICE LINES (Examples)
----------------------
- When player collects resource: "Ooh, shiny!" / "Gimme that!"
- When recycling: "Mmm, delicious garbage!" / "Processing... processing... done!"
- When player takes damage: "Ouch! That looked painful!" / "You okay there, buddy?"
- Idle comments: Random funny observations about the environment
- Encouragement: Sarcastic but supportive remarks

ANIMATION IDEAS
---------------
- Idle hover animation (gentle floating)
- Happy/excited animation when receiving resources
- Processing animation (internal mechanisms working)
- Face expressions changing based on context
- Following movement (smooth tracking of player)
